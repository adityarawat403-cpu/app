ReplyShastra Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Overview

ReplyShastra is an AI-powered reply suggestion tool. We respect user privacy and design the app to minimize collection of personal information.

2. Message Processing

When you type a message, the text is processed only to generate a suggested reply. Conversations are not publicly visible and are not shared with other users.

3. Account Information

If you create an account, we may store basic identifiers such as email address for login, security, and subscription management purposes.

4. Data Usage

We may analyze anonymized and non-identifiable usage data to improve reply quality, performance, and system reliability. Personal conversations are not manually reviewed or exposed publicly.

5. Data Storage

ReplyShastra does not publish or sell user conversations. Messages may be temporarily processed for generating replies but are not displayed to other users.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. User Control

You may stop using the service at any time. Avoid entering highly sensitive personal or financial information into the application.

8. Third-Party Services

Certain features (login, analytics, or hosting) may rely on secure third-party infrastructure providers necessary to operate the service.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as features evolve. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
